## 🧠 My Methodology

Every writeup follows this structure:

1. **Reconnaissance** — scanning ports, finding services
1. **Enumeration** — digging deeper into open services
1. **Exploitation** — finding and using vulnerabilities
1. **Post-Exploitation / Flags** — capturing objectives
1. **Lessons Learned** — what I took away from the challenge
